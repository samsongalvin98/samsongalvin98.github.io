Beer Olympics

Game Rules

  • Beer Ball: Teams throw ping pong balls at beer cans. If you hit, drink until the ball is returned. First team to finish wins.
  • Musical Flip Cup: Like musical chairs, but with flip cup. When music stops, last to flip is out.
  • Fuck Yeah: Players take turns saying "fuck yeah" in a circle. If you mess up, drink. Last standing wins.
  • Beer Darts: Throw darts at beer cans. If you puncture a can, the owner drinks. Points for accuracy.
  • Stack Cup: Bounce a ball into a cup, then stack it. Last player with unstacked cup loses.
